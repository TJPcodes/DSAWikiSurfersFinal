About the Program

Surfing Wikipedia is like driving a web of hyperlinks: some routes are direct and flat, others are circuitous. Just as different wave conditions affect the way a surfer rides across the ocean, different algorithmic strategies affect the way a search can find its target efficiently. Our project will study and compare search algorithms to identify the optimal approach to connecting topics in large-scale knowledge graphs. These observations can be applied to AI search engines, web spiders, and scalable network traversals.
